# Flourish-Face

> An award-winning iOS app for gentle facial movement practice, built with AR face tracking, daily progress, and a flower-based reward loop.

**Swift Student Challenge Distinguished Winner**

## Overview

Flourish-Face is an iOS app designed around small, consistent facial movement practice. It combines AR-based face tracking with a gentle daily routine, helping users follow exercises, track their progress, and collect flowers as a visual reward for consistency.

The app is built as a Swift Package-based iOS project and focuses on a calm, supportive experience rather than a clinical or medical claim.

## Why Flourish-Face?

Some days, even practicing a small facial movement can feel emotionally difficult.

Flourish-Face approaches that experience with a softer interaction model: short daily sessions, visual progress, and a growth metaphor that turns consistency into something users can see and collect.

## Key Features

- **Personalized onboarding**
  Set the affected side and preferred difficulty before starting the routine.

- **AR-guided facial movement practice**
  Uses AR face tracking to support interactive facial exercise sessions.

- **Daily exercise routine**
  Encourages small, consistent practice through a simple daily flow.

- **Progress and growth tracking**
  Tracks completed sessions and visualizes progress over time.

- **Flower collection reward loop**
  Rewards consistency with collectible flowers and a gentle growth metaphor.

- **Localized experience**
  Includes localization resources for a more accessible app experience.

## App Flow

1. Start with a short introduction to the app's purpose.
2. Choose the affected side and preferred difficulty.
3. Follow daily facial movement exercises.
4. Track progress through logs and growth indicators.
5. Collect flowers as a reward for consistency.
